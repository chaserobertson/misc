Lab 10: Neural Networkds and Deep Learning

The purpose of this lab is to get experience with neural networks and deep learning.

It is strongly recommended that you complete this lab on the VMs, but it can perhaps also be completed on any computer that has R (version >= 4.0) available. Your final R markdown document must be processed on the VMs to produce the HTML document for submission.
